Government-Recognized Certifications from Learning Tree

Cost-Effective and on the GSA's IT and Professional Services Schedules

As a leading provider of IT training to the federal government (GSA's IT 70 and Professional Services Schedules) as well as state and local governments and qualified federal contractors, Learning Tree has the experience and expertise to deliver agency-specific, cost-effective solutions to help you meet your certification objectives.

Earn National Association of State Boards of Accountancy (NASBA) Credits

Earn CPE credits from the National Association of State Boards of Accountancy CPE program through Learning Tree courses.

Learning Tree International is registered with the National Association of State Boards of Accountancy (NASBA) as a sponsor of continuing professional education on the National Registry of CPE Sponsors. State boards of accountancy have final authority on the acceptance of individual courses for CPE credit.

Complaints regarding registered sponsors may be addressed to the National Registry of CPE Sponsors, 150 Fourth Avenue North, Suite 700, Nashville, TN 37219-2417.
